Road Construction

The major responsibility of the Nagar Palika Parishad in terms of road construction is to construct roads and bridges in their defined area using highest quality of materials and technology enabling proper management of roadways and traffic in their defined jurisdiction area. Certainly, Nagar Palika Parishad works for planning, Design, Implementation & Monitoring of various Road and Bridge Construction work within its jurisdiction.
